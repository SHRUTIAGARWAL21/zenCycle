# zenCycle

zenCycle is a comprehensive mood and task tracker designed to help users improve their productivity and well-being. Built with Next.js, it offers a seamless and modern experience for tracking daily moods, managing tasks, and gaining actionable insights.

## Key Features

- **Mood Tracking:** Log your daily mood and visualize trends over time.
- **Task Management:** Add, update, and track tasks to stay organized and productive.
- **Productivity Insights:** Get personalized productivity tips and feedback powered by the Gemini API.
- **Streak System:** Stay motivated with a LeetCode-style streak system that rewards consistency.
- **Productivity Graph:** Visualize your progress with a GitHub-style productivity graph.
- **Journaling:** Add notes and reflections to each day with a built-in journaling feature.
- **Notifications:** Receive timely reminders and notifications to help you stay engaged and consistent.

## How It Works

1. **Log Your Mood:** Quickly record how you're feeling each day.
2. **Manage Tasks:** Add new tasks, mark them as complete, and track your daily progress.
3. **View Insights:** Access personalized productivity insights and tips generated by the Gemini API.
4. **Track Streaks:** Build and maintain streaks to boost your motivation.
5. **Reflect:** Use the journaling feature to add notes and reflect on your day.
6. **Stay Notified:** Get reminders to log your mood and complete tasks, helping you build positive habits.
